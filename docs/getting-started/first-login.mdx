# First Login Guide

This guide will walk you through your initial setup and explains the local-first experience.

    *   **Note:** Your scripts, presets, and data remain entirely on your local machine.

## ðŸš€ Verify Connection

To ensure everything is working correctly:

1.  Launch **Autodesk Revit**.
2.  Navigate to the **Paracore** tab in the ribbon.
3.  Click the **Paracore Server** button (it will toggle from **Off** to **On**).
4.  Confirm the popup message mentioning *"Listening on port 50051"*.
5.  Launch the **Paracore Desktop App** and verify the **Connected** status in the top bar.
