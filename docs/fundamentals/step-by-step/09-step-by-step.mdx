# 09 - Step-by-Step: Paracore Sentinels

Practice deploying and managing background BIM watchdogs.

## 1. Pre-Flight Check
1. Select a Sentinel script (e.g., a "Room Name Case Checker") in the Gallery.
2. Adjust the parameters in the UI.
3. Click **Run** manually to verify the output in the Console.

## 2. Deploy Background Sentinel
1. Open the **Sidebar**, click the **Settings (Gear)**, and go to the **Sentinels** tab.
2. Click the **Deploy toggle** for your script. Notice the "Snapshot Mechanism" locks in the current values.

## 3. Test Background Reaction
1. Switch to Revit and perform an action that violates the Sentinel's rules.
2. Observe the notification or the **Sentinel Control Window**.

## 4. Independent Updates
1. Go back to the **Gallery** and change the script parameters.
2. Click **Run** manually and notice the output differs from the background Sentinel.
3. To update the background watchdog, **Undeploy** and then **Re-deploy**.

## 5. Lifecycle Management
1. Close Paracore and notice the Sentinels undeploy (Resource Protection).
2. Reopen Paracore and use the **Deploy All** button in Sentinel Settings to restore your watchdogs.
