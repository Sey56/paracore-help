## ü§ñ Advanced Automation (Under Development)

We are working on powerful new modes to orchestrate your automation library.

### Agentic Automation
Go beyond single-script execution. The AI Agent will be able to orchestrate multiple scripts to perform complex, multi-step tasks (e.g., "Analyze the model, identify missing rooms, and place them on the correct levels").

### Playlist Mode
The ability to queue and execute a sequence of scripts in a specific order, allowing for complex, standardized BIM workflows that can be shared across teams.
