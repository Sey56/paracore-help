# Welcome to Paracore: Revit Automation Platform

The Revit Automation Platform (Paracore) is a powerful and intuitive tool designed to help AEC (Architecture, Engineering, and Construction) firms create, manage, and dynamically execute C# scripts to automate tasks within Autodesk Revit.

Paracore leverages a unique **hybrid model** that combines the speed and security of local execution with the collaborative power of cloud services. This means your scripts run directly on your machine for maximum performance and data privacy, while team management and collaboration features are seamlessly handled through cloud-connected services.

## What You Can Do with Paracore

*   **Automate Revit Tasks:** Run custom C# scripts to streamline repetitive or complex operations in Revit.
*   **Collaborate with Your Team:** Share and manage scripts with your team members using a Git-powered workflow.
*   **Manage Script Parameters:** Easily define and adjust script inputs without touching code.
*   **Ensure Quality:** Utilize role-based permissions and Git best practices to maintain a high standard for your automation scripts.

This documentation will guide you through installing Paracore, understanding its core features, collaborating with your team, and leveraging its full potential to enhance your Revit workflows.
